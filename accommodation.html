Four Points by Sheraton

9,000 to 15,000 PHP

A 20-minute boat ride from the beautiful Subterranean Underground River, Four Points by Sheraton Palawan Puerto Princesa enjoys a quiet location on Sabang Beach in Puerto Princesa City. An outdoor pool and pampering spa treatments are available. The spacious and air conditioning rooms also feature a flat-screen TV, personal safe, and mini-bar. Private bathrooms come with either a bathtub or shower. Four Points by Sheraton Palawan Puerto Princesa is a 90-minute drive from Puerto Princesa International Airport. It is a 5-minute walk from Sabang Wharf. A relaxing massage and other pampering treatments can be enjoyed at the spa. A 24/7 fitness center and kids club are also provided. The beachfront Evolution all-day dining restaurant features local and international fusions with farm-fresh ingredients and healthy options. The Pool Bar are other venues is another venue where one can relax while sipping on their favorite drink.

El Nido Resorts Lagen Island

174,593 to 192,688 PHP

Set in a cove bordered by thick forests, Lagen Island Resort is located in beautiful El Nido, Palawan. An on-site marine sports center offers activities like diving, kayaking. Resort activities include hiking, snorkeling, and mangrove or cave tours. The resort also has an outdoor pool, a spa, and a library. Newly renovated rooms feature antique wood floors, reclaimed wood furnishings, a CD player, and mini-bar. Floor-to-ceiling glass doors open up to a private balcony overlooking the cove, bay, or cliffs. Each room has a private bathroom with shower facilities and bath amenities. Guests can go hiking through Lagen Island's forest trail, which leads to a view of one of the coves on the other side of the island. Lagen Island Resort can be reached via a 75-minute charter flight from Manila to El Nido (LIO) Airport. Free transfers are provided from El Nido Airport, which is a 50-minute boat ride to the resort.

Want a great night's sleep? This property was highly rated for its very comfortable beds.

Two Seasons Coron Resort

25,764 to 38,000 PHP

The 5-star Two Seasons Island Resort is a beachfront property located on Coron, Palawan. It features luxurious bungalows, a restaurant, and a variety of recreational facilities. A free roundtrip airport transfer is provided, along with complimentary rental for select non-motorized water sports equipment, and breakfast. All units are equipped with a private balcony, sofa seating area and a flat-screen cable TV. Guests can relax on the private beach, windsurf, banana boat or go on a diving trip. Other recreational choices include playing a game of billiards or indulging in a massage at the spa. The restaurant serves a spread of local and international dishes. Refreshing beverages and light snacks are offered at the bar. Two Seasons Coron Island Resort is accessible from Busuanga Airport via a 30-minute drive followed by a 45-minute boat ride.

Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 9.5 for a two-person trip.
