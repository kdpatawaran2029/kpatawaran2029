El Nido

Known for its dramatic limestone cliffs and clear turquoise waters, El Nido is a top tourist destination in Palawan.

Coron

Coron features stunning lakes, World War II wreck dives, and breathtaking mountain views.

Puerto Princesa Underground River

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this underground river flows beneath a limestone karst mountain.

Nacpan Beach

A pristine 4-km white sand beach near El Nido, perfect for relaxation and sunset views.

Tubbataha Reefs

A remote UNESCO-listed marine park known for diving, colorful coral reefs, and abundant marine life.
